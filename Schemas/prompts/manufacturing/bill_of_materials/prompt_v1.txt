- parent_part_description (string): Parent part description.
- effective_date (date): BOM effective date.
- plant_or_site (string): Plant, site, or manufacturing location.
- engineering_change_order (string): Related engineering change order.
- component_items (array<object>): Component list with part number, quantity, unit, revision, and scrap factor.

- approval_date (date): Approval date.
- bom_status (string): Released, draft, obsolete, pending, or other status.

- 1.0: Explicit labeled field with no ambiguity.
- 0.90-0.99: Clearly present with minor normalization.

- 0.60-0.74: Ambiguous or partially legible.
