- supplier_id (string): Supplier identifier.
- commodity_category (string): Commodity, material group, or category.

- quality_ppm (number): Defective parts per million or quality PPM.
- nonconformance_count (integer): Number of nonconformances.
- cost_variance_percent (number): Cost variance percentage.
- responsiveness_rating (string): Responsiveness rating.
- overall_score (number): Overall score.
- supplier_tier (string): Preferred, approved, conditional, probation, disqualified, or other tier.
- corrective_actions_open (array<string>): Open corrective actions.
- review_owner (string): Owner of supplier review.
- next_review_date (date): Next review date.

Confidence scoring:
- 1.0: Explicit labeled field with no ambiguity.
- 0.90-0.99: Clearly present with minor normalization.
- 0.75-0.89: Present but requires reading across nearby context.
- 0.60-0.74: Ambiguous or partially legible.
- 0.0: Missing or not reliable.
