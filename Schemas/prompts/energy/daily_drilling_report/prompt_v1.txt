- rig_name (string): Rig name or rig number.
- contractor_name (string): Drilling contractor or rig contractor.
- location (string): County, state, block, lease, or offshore location.
- measured_depth (number): Measured depth at report cutoff.
- true_vertical_depth (number): True vertical depth at report cutoff.
- daily_footage (number): Footage drilled during the reporting period.
- hole_size (string): Hole size for current interval.
- mud_weight (number): Mud weight in pounds per gallon when stated.
- casing_status (string): Casing, cementing, or liner status.
- operations_summary (string): Narrative summary of rig operations for the reporting day.
- downtime_hours (number): Non-productive time or downtime hours.
- safety_incidents (integer): Count of safety incidents for the day.
- hse_summary (string): HSE notes or safety summary.
- daily_cost (number): Estimated or reported daily drilling cost.
- company_representative (string): Company man, drilling supervisor, or report approver.

- 1.0: Explicit labeled field with no ambiguity.

- 0.75-0.89: Present but requires reading across nearby context.
